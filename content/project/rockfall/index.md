We developed a stability preserving time integration scheme for rockfall simulations. With this work, we combined Lyapunov stability theory with event-capturing methods to develop a time integration scheme that preserves the stability of the rocks during free flight. This was a collaboration with the Institute for Snow and Avalanche Research (SLF) in Davos (CH).
